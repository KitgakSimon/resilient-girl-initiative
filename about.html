More About Our Initiative

We believe that every Girl child has the capacity to win and Navigate against barriers. We are committed to redesigning the future of young girls in the society through organizing of measurable social impact campaign and leadership projects that will affect girlsâ€™ lives, and her community.

Resilient Girl Initiative is a Girls-Focused Non-profit Organization founded in October 2018. we have helped and enable Girls to break every Societal stereotype that affect their lives. and we have impacted over 20,000 girl and women across Nigeria and Africa .

We are committed on Four key projects, which are addressing Sexual and Gender-based Violence, ending period poverty, providing Self-esteem and leadership mentorship program for young Secondary and universities girls and Sustainable Development Goals interventional projects.

Our Mission

Resilient Girl Initiative is a Feminist focused organization, our mission is to transform the future of young girls in Nigeria and across Africa by helping girls to navigate out of life challenges with confidence and self-esteem through mentorship impactful social campaign projects that address issues of sexual and gender-based violence period poverty, leadership and sustainable development practices.

Our Vision

Our is to Ensure that every girls have Confident in their life choices to pursue their dream, knowing what it takes to excel, with potentiality to Navigate against odds and to Win in every areas of life
