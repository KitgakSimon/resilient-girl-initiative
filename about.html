Our Mission

Resilient Girl Initiative is a Feminist focused organization, our mission is to transform the future of young girls in Nigeria and across Africa by helping girls to navigate out of life challenges with confidence and self-esteem through mentorship impactful social campaign projects that address issues of sexual and gender-based violence period poverty, leadership and sustainable development practices.


Vision

Our vision is to ensure that every girl has Confidence in their life choices to pursue their dreams and speak out against all forms of social injustices that imbed her life.


OBJECTIVES

Our Objectives is to:

  • provide leadership mentorship for girls
  • provide mental health care support services to girls and women,
  • provide legal support for victims of sexual and gender-based violence
  • address period poverty
  • provide second chance education for young girls.
  • empower women and girls with financial resource and organize community based social impact campaign project in promoting Gender equality
