Mission

Mission

Resilient Girl Initiative is a Feminist focused organization, our mission is to transform the future of young girls in Nigeria and across Africa by helping girls to navigate out of life challenges with confidence and self-esteem through mentorship impactful social campaign projects that address issues of sexual and gender-based violence period poverty, leadership and sustainable development practices.

Our vision

Vision

Our vision is to Ensure that all girls have Confident in their life choices to pursue their dream, knowing what it takes to excel, with potentiality to navigate against odds and to Win in every areas of life and stand against all forms of social injustices.
